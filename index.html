Terms and Conditions

Promoter Investment: The promoter contributes ₹25,000 to the company in exchange for 1 share.

Annual Dividend or ROI: The promoter receives either:

  • An annual dividend based on the company’s performance (at least 15% of the investment), or
  • A fixed 15% return on their initial investment, whichever is higher.

Lock-in Period: The promoter cannot sell or transfer their shares for 12 months.

Company Buyback Option: The company has the right to buy back the promoter’s shares anytime.
